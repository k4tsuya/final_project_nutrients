Grilled Salmon Bowl

Healthy, balanced & nutrient-packed!

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 wild-caught salmon fillets (approx. 150-180g each)
  • 185g cooked quinoa (approx. 1 cup)
  • 100g broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon (3g) garlic powder
  • Juice of 1 lemon (approx. 2 tablespoons / 30ml)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Toss the broccoli, bell pepper, and zucchini with 1 tbsp (15ml) olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, season the salmon with salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Grill or pan-sear for 3-4 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  3. To serve, layer the quinoa in a bowl, top with the roasted vegetables and grilled salmon. Drizzle with remaining olive oil and garnish with parsley.

Nutrition Highlights

  • Rich in Omega-3 fatty acids (from salmon)
  • High in fiber, iron, and magnesium (from quinoa)
  • Excellent source of vitamins A, C, and K (from vegetables)
  • Balanced macronutrients: lean protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates
